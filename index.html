Lodhran Public School.

Lodhran Public School (LPS), run by the Tareen Education Foundation (TEF), is a school in Lodhran, Pakistan, offering a private, internationally-standard education, particularly focused on preparing students for CAIE O Level and A Level examinations.

Quality Education

Modern Infrastructure

Lodhran Public School features a renovated building and new facilities, including a digital learning center, a well-equipped library, science laboratories, an art room, and a conference room.

Technology Integration

The school is supported by the Tareen Education Foundation (TEF), which focuses on improving education through IT, as evidenced by the establishment of a Digital Learning Centre and upgrades to computer laboratories.

Public-Private Collaboration

The school operates in collaboration with the Tareen Education Foundation and the Education Department, Punjab, ensuring a strong foundation for educational standards.

Student-Centered Environment

The school aims to create a dynamic and supportive learning environment where students can develop their full potential.
